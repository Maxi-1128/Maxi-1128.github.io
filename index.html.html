What is Hogwarts?

Hogwarts is a magical school for young witches and wizards in or near the United Kingdom.

Where is Hogwarts?

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is located in the Scottish Highlands, nestled between two mountains—Ben Nevis and Ben Macdui—and situated in a massive castle. The precise location is protected by powerful spells, making it invisible to Muggles.

When to Attend Hogwarts

Students begin attending Hogwarts at age 11 and continue for seven years. Term starts on September 1st each year.

How to Go to Hogwarts

Students typically travel to Hogwarts via the Hogwarts Express, which departs from Platform 9¾ at King's Cross Station in London. Every year on September 1st, students board the train at 11 a.m.

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw values intelligence, learning, wisdom, and wit.

Slytherin

House Points System

Points are awarded or deducted by professors based on student behavior. At the end of the year, the house with the most points wins the House Cup. Triumphs will earn points, while misbehaving actions will lose points.

Uniform and Supplies

All students must wear standard Hogwarts robes. Supplies are purchased from Diagon Alley before term starts. Each student must bring a wand, cauldron, set of books, and other items. See entire list in your Hogwarts Letter.

Quidditch at Hogwarts

Quidditch is the favorite sport at Hogwarts. Each house has a team, and matches are held throughout the school year. The team with the most wins takes home the Quidditch Cup.
